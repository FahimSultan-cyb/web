About Me

Distinguished researcher advancing computational frontiers in artificial intelligence and information systems

I am a dedicated PhD student in Computer Science and Engineering at Oakland University, specializing in Information Technology with a focus on cutting-edge computational research. My scholarly pursuits encompass artificial intelligence, machine learning, cybersecurity, and quantum computing, where I develop innovative solutions that bridge theoretical foundations with transformative real-world applications.

My academic philosophy centers on rigorous scientific inquiry and collaborative excellence. I am committed to contributing meaningful advances to the global research community through high-impact publications, interdisciplinary collaboration, and mentoring emerging scholars. My work addresses critical challenges in modern computing while exploring the frontiers of what computational science can achieve for society.
